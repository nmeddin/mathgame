About our Mission

Mathematics anxiety has been defined as feelings of tension and anxiety that interfere with the manipulation of numbers and the solving of mathematical problems in a wide variety of ordinary life and academic situations. Elementary and middle students are most susceptible to math anxiety. This affects their academic confidence, and by causal connection, their performance. When teachers are the main sources of this anxiety, students can look to outside sources such as our math game. Math Wars improves math skills, fosters cooperation, and most importantly, it provides them a comfortable environment to help them develop.
